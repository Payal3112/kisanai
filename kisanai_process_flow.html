Process Flow: From Satellite Data to Farmer Advisory

End-to-End Processing Pipeline

1

Satellite Images (Sentinel-2)

Multi-spectral optical imagery from Copernicus Sentinel-2 satellites providing 10-20m resolution data across visible, near-infrared, and shortwave infrared bands.

Sentinel-2 10-20m resolution Multi-spectral
2

Image Preprocessing

Atmospheric correction, cloud masking, geometric correction, and temporal compositing to prepare raw satellite data for analysis.

Atmospheric correction Cloud masking Temporal compositing
3

Feature Extraction

Calculation of key vegetation and moisture indices: NDVI (vegetation health), NDWI (water content), EVI (enhanced vegetation index), and other relevant indices.

NDVI NDWI EVI Vegetation indices
4

Crop Classification

Machine learning models classify crop types based on spectral-temporal patterns in the extracted features, enabling automatic crop identification.

ML classification Spectral-temporal Crop types
5

Crop Health Analysis

Analysis of vegetation indices over time to assess crop vigor, growth stage determination, and health scoring based on phenological patterns.

Time-series analysis Growth stage Health scoring
6

Weather Integration

Integration of forecast data (rainfall, temperature, humidity) from sources like NASA POWER or OpenWeatherMap to complement satellite observations.

Weather forecasts NASA POWER OpenWeatherMap
7

Moisture Stress Detection

Combination of satellite-derived moisture indices (NDWI, LSWI) with weather data to detect and quantify crop water stress conditions.

NDWI LSWI Water stress
8

Water Deficit Estimation

Calculation of crop-specific water requirements vs. available water (from rainfall and soil moisture) to estimate irrigation water deficit.

Water requirement Soil moisture Irrigation deficit
9

AI Recommendation Engine

Artificial intelligence models generate personalized recommendations for irrigation scheduling, fertilizer application, and crop management based on all integrated data.

AI/ML models Irrigation scheduling Fertilizer advice
10

Farmer Dashboard

Web-based interface displaying crop health maps, time-series graphs, stress alerts, and historical advisory data for informed decision-making.

Web dashboard Crop health map Time-series graphs
11

Voice/SMS Alerts

Delivery of timely alerts and recommendations via voice calls or SMS notifications to ensure farmers receive critical information promptly.

Voice alerts SMS notifications Timely delivery
