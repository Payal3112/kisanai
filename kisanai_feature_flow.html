KisanAI

Satellite-Driven Crop Digital Twin for Intelligent Crop Monitoring

Core Features

  • 🌱

    Satellite Crop Mapping

    Automatic crop identification and multi-season crop monitoring using satellite imagery

  • 📊

    Crop Health Monitoring

    NDVI-based vegetation monitoring, moisture stress detection, and crop health scoring

  • 🌦️

    Weather Intelligence

    Rainfall forecasting, temperature monitoring, and water deficit estimation

  • 🤖

    AI Advisory Engine

    Provides recommendations for irrigation scheduling, water stress alerts, and crop management suggestions

  • 💡

    Explainable AI

    Every recommendation includes confidence score, contributing factors, and satellite evidence

  • 📱

    Farmer Dashboard

    Crop health map, time-series graphs, stress alerts, and advisory history

  • 🔮

    Future Features

    Voice assistant, WhatsApp/SMS alerts, disease detection using leaf images, ground sensor integration
